Nernst Equation

The Nernst potential is directly proportional to temperature, and so the Nernst potential should decrease with the decrease in temperature.

Gibbs Free Energy

Gibbs free energy can be described as the amount of energy available to do work. This can be associated with changes of both enthalpy and entropy (ΔH, ΔS), these changes decide how favorable a reaction can be. free energy of a reaction is defined as: ΔG = ΔH - T ΔS
